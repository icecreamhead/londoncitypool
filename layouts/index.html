Welcome to London City Pool League

About The League

Established in 1992, the London City Pool League was set up to allow businesses the opportunity to enter pool teams in an independent league. It is still strongly associated with the business community, however we are now open to all that want to play competitive 8 ball pool in London.

The London City Pool League is primarily based in the city, with teams playing out of pubs and snooker clubs all around central London. If you are looking to play competitive 8 ball pool in central London, get your cue! Teams are six players (but you can register more). Find a home venue or simply use one of the existing ones. The venue must have at least two good quality 8 ball pool tables. Contact us for further details.
